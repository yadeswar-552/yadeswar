Ways to tour Paris

Book these experiences for a close-up look at Paris.

Louvre Museum Timed Entry Ticket - Optional Private Guided Tour

Explore the renowned Louvre Museum in Paris and behold the world's most celebrated painting, the Mona Lisa, in person. Additionally, witness numerous other artworks.

Eiffel Tower Access to the Second Floor and the Summit by Elevator

Start your tour of the Eiffel Tower by meeting your guide, who will give you an introduction to the tower and the history of its construction.

Paris Seine River Dinner Cruise with Live Music by Bateaux Mouches

See the iconic sights of Paris from the water, illuminated for the evening, on this Bateaux Mouches sightseeing dinner cruise.

Paris Walking Food Tour with Secret Food Tours

Enjoying Paris cuisine is a highlight of any trip to the French capital, but finding authentic versions of pastries, cheeses, and breads can be a challenge.

French Wine and Champagne Tasting in Paris

France is renowned around the world for its fine wines—but thanks to the language barrier, it can be hard to learn more about them. This wine and Champagne tasting experience—which includes six tastings.

Paris en Scene 3 Course Seine River Dinner Cruise

The City of Light is most magical by night, and a dinner cruise along the Seine is a quintessential experience for Paris visitors.

Disneyland-Paris

Welcome to the place where everyone can shine their brightest Since opening in 1992.

River-Seine

Bodies of Water, Hop on a river cruise or stroll alongside this famous waterway—it flows through the heart of Paris and separates the city's left and right banks.
